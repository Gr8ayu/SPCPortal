About

A little bit about our organization

R V College of Engineering is one of the top colleges in India with almost around 95% of the eligible students are placed through campus selection.

This Career Portal aims to provide information on companies offering internships or jobs so that students may view and assess their opportunities.

  • To provide a platform for student database management for placement related activities.
  • To provide the students as well as Student Placement Coordinators and concerned academic staff the day to day placement details in the form of reports.
  • To assist in keeping track of students and companies involved in the internship and placement activities.

RVCE has consistently maintained an excellent recruitment record. The graduates and post- graduates of RVCE have been recruited by some of the world's leading corporate, besides leading Indian companies.
